This is an Etali, (his friends called him Steve) a long forgotten evolution of dinosaur. Due to his strange leg anatomy, it is often speculated that the Etali species is a distant relative of the giraffe. Regardless, Steve is capable of jumping extremely high, running extremely fast, and being absolutely, unabashedly, the coolest dino-man a person could have the pleasure of meeting. Careful though, his long legs make him quite clumsy so he is prone to falling over.
